Order Tracking System

Welcome to the Order Tracking System! This project, developed during a summer training program, addresses inefficiencies in the order flow within the company by providing a streamlined solution for order management.

The system empowers users to seamlessly add and track orders, providing real-time updates on their status. Departments can easily manage order states, significantly enhancing overall operational efficiency.

Our integrated API allows users to check order statuses instantly without waiting for replies, ensuring a transparent and user-friendly experience. This is just the first feature, with many more enhancements planned for the future to make our system even more robust and efficient.
